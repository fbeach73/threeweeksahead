My Story

I had no idea anything was wrong.

In July 2025 I was clearing trees at the lake. My left arm started aching. I told myself it was a pinched nerve. Four days later I was admitted to the hospital. A week after that, a cardiac surgeon in Saint John told me three of the arteries to my heart were almost completely blocked.

Ten days later I drove back for a double bypass.

I've spent the months since recovering, walking, and answering questions for other people just starting this road. That's what this channel is. Someone who walked it a few weeks ahead of you, turning around to help you do the same.

— Kyle
